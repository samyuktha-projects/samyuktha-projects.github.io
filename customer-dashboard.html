About RFM Analysis

RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) analysis is a data-driven customer segmentation technique. It evaluates customers based on: Recency (how recently they purchased), Frequency (how often they purchase), and Monetary (how much they spend). This helps identify valuable customers and optimize marketing strategies.
